Windspeed

In terms of windspeed, the overall calmest part of the earth is along the equator. The heaviest winds seem to be the furthest from the equators, along the -55S and 65N latitudes. The heaviest winds, at 49N, were just over 35 MPH. The northern hemisphere does have more statistical diversity regarding windspeed, and the south seems to be somewhat calmer.
